Name

book of allies - Find a company at GHC!

Points

5 points

Type

Physical

Description

We've sent an operative to the 2017 Grace Hopper Celebration, but the registration staff insisted they could only issue badges to humans. We got a tip that one of the speakers' companies might be willing to help our cause, but we don't know which company to contact and we can't risk alerting Evil Robot Corp. Can you help us?
